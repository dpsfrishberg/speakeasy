What is Speakeasy?

Welcome!

Speakeasy lets you discuss as you read and work your way through a text, unlike normal comment systems that dump all the comments into one giant bucket at the end.

Additionally, when you comment using Speakeasy you can type just like you talk and think with your friends. Press 'enter' whenever you finish a thought and the opportunity to respond in a new tangent is created, just like a normal conversation.

We don't have conversations in real life in a line, why should we online? Great conversations can't be contained or mapped into a structure - they are organic and complex. Speakeasy lets you comment in a way that makes room for conversations that can take the twists, tangents and asides that make great conversations memorable.
